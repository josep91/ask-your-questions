Come Ask Your Questions!

Hi! I'm sure you have a lot of questions. This website will help you in finding your own answers. How do you do that?

First, write down your questions. Upload a text (.txt) file with your questions.


Second, find your answers. Here are some ways you can do that:
  • Pray. James 1:5 in the Bible reads, "If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him."
  • Visit ChurchofJesusChrist.org and ComeUntoChrist.org.
  • Ask your parents for advice.
  • For technical or knowledge-based questions, try asking a teacher, looking in a book, or using a Google search.
  • If you don't know the answer, it's okay to put "I don't know."
